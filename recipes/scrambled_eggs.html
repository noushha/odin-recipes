Scrambled Eggs

Description

Scrambled eggs is a dish made from eggs that are stirred, whipped, or beaten together typically with salt, butter, oil, and sometimes other ingredients, and fried.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 eggs
  • butter
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. crack eggs and stir until fully mixed
  2. add in melted butter to eggs and mix again
  3. add mixture to hot pan with more butter
  4. continue stirring once mixture is in the pan until it is soft and fluffy
  5. add salt and pepper
