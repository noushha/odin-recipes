Omelette

Description

In cuisine, an omelette (also spelled omelet) is a dish made from beaten eggs, fried with butter or oil in a frying pan (without stirring as in scrambled egg).

It is quite common for the omelette to be folded around fillings such as chives, vegetables, mushrooms, meat (often ham or bacon), cheese, onions or some combination of the above.

Ingredients

  • Eggs (2)
  • melted butter (1tbs)
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • shredded cheese
  • spinach

Steps

  1. Crack the two eggs and beat them with salt and pepper
  2. Add melted butter and continue to stir
  3. Heat up a pan and add butter. Add the mixture when pan is hot
  4. Add the shredded cheese, and spinach
  5. Flip over the side of the omelette when it turns golden
