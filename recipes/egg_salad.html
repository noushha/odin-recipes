Egg Salad

Description

Egg salad is a dish made primarily of chopped hard-boiled or scrambled eggs, mustard, and mayonnaise, and vegetables often including other ingredients such as celery.

It is made mixed with seasonings in the form of herbs, spices and other ingredients, bound with mayonnaise.

Ingredients

  • 4 hardboiled eggs
  • chives
  • mayonnaise
  • mustard
  • celery
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. use fork to mash up the eggs in a bowl
  2. add in the chopped celery
  3. add chives
  4. fold in mayonnaise and mustard and mix
  5. add salt and pepper to taste
